## Background

In accordance with **Royal Decree-Law 13/2012, of March 30**, which regulates the use of cookies in electronic communications services, we inform users about the use of cookies on the **Gilsys** website.

By accessing this website, the user consents to the use of **analytics cookies** for collecting statistical data in an anonymous manner.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small **text files** stored on the user's device when they visit a website. They allow **information to be collected** about browsing behavior and, in some cases, improve the user's experience.

The cookies used on this website **do not collect personal information** nor do they allow user identification.

## Types of cookies used

This website only uses **analytics cookies** managed by **Google Analytics** through **Google Tag Manager**. Their purpose is to measure and analyze website traffic, as well as user behavior, in an **anonymous and aggregated** manner.

### **Cookies used**
| **Provider** | **Purpose** | **Description** |
|--------------|------------|----------------|
| Google (Analytics) | Website traffic analysis | Collects anonymous data about website visits to improve the browsing experience. |

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the website, allowing us to optimize its content and functionality.

## Managing and deleting cookies

Users can **configure their browser** to accept, block, or delete cookies at any time. Below are links with instructions for the most popular browsers:

- **Google Chrome** → [Manage cookies in Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en)
- **Mozilla Firefox** → [Manage cookies in Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
- **Microsoft Edge** → [Manage cookies in Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4027947/microsoft-edge-delete-cookies)
- **Safari (Mac, iPhone, iPad)** → [Manage cookies in Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac)
- **Opera** → [Manage cookies in Opera](https://help.opera.com/en/latest/web-preferences/#cookies)

If the user chooses to **block all cookies**, the website will continue to function correctly, but there may be **limitations in statistical data collection**.

## Changes to the cookies policy

Gilsys reserves the right to modify this **cookies policy** to adapt it to **legal or technical updates**. We recommend reviewing it periodically to stay informed about how and why cookies are used.
