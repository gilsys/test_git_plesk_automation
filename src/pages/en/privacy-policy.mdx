## Background

In accordance with **Royal Decree-Law 13/2012, of March 30**, which regulates the use of cookies in electronic communication services, we inform users about the use of cookies on the **Gilsys** website.

By accessing this website, the user consents to the use of **essential and analytics cookies** to ensure the proper functioning of the site and the anonymous collection of statistical data.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small **text files** stored on the user's device when visiting a website. They allow the collection of **information** about browsing activity and, in some cases, improve the user experience.

The cookies used on this website **do not collect personal information** and do not allow user identification.

## Types of cookies used

This website uses the following types of cookies:

### **Strictly Necessary Cookies**
These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the website and cannot be disabled. They do not store personally identifiable information and are used to remember user preferences and improve the browsing experience.

| **Name**     | **Provider** | **Purpose**                                 | **Duration** |
|--------------|--------------|-----------------------------------------------|-------------|
| cc_cookie    | Gilsys       | Saves preferences regarding cookie usage. | 6 months |
| lang         | Gilsys       | Remembers the user’s selected language. | 6 months |

### **Analytics Cookies**
These cookies help measure web traffic and analyze user behavior in an anonymous and aggregated manner. They are managed through **Google Analytics** via **Google Tag Manager**.

| **Name**     | **Provider**         | **Purpose**                                | **Duration** |
|--------------|----------------------|--------------------------------------------|-------------|
| _ga          | Google Analytics     | Identifies users anonymously for visit analysis. | 2 years |
| _gid         | Google Analytics     | Differentiates between users to analyze behavior on the site. | 24 hours |
| _gat         | Google Analytics     | Limits request rates to Google Analytics. | 1 minute |
| _gcl_au      | Google Tag Manager   | Stores conversion tracking information. | 3 months |

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the website, allowing us to optimize its content and functionality.

## Managing and Deleting Cookies

Users can **configure their browser** to accept, block, or delete cookies at any time. Below are links with instructions for the most popular browsers:

- **Google Chrome** → [Manage cookies in Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en)
- **Mozilla Firefox** → [Manage cookies in Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
- **Microsoft Edge** → [Manage cookies in Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4027947/microsoft-edge-delete-cookies)
- **Safari (Mac, iPhone, iPad)** → [Manage cookies in Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac)
- **Opera** → [Manage cookies in Opera](https://help.opera.com/en/latest/web-preferences/#cookies)

If the user **blocks all cookies**, the website will still function correctly, but some **limitations in statistical data collection** may occur.

Gilsys reserves the right to modify this **cookie policy** to adapt it to **legal or technical developments**. We recommend reviewing it periodically to stay informed about how and why cookies are used.
